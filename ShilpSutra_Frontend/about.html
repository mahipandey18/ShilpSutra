Our Vision

ShilpSutra aims to bridge the gap between tradition and technology. By leveraging AI, we empower artisans and buyers to verify the authenticity of Indian handicrafts, ensuring that the legacy of our "Parampara" (Tradition) stands the test of "Pramaan" (Proof).
